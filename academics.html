Academics

Early Childhood

Overview

Our early childhood program focuses on providing a nurturing and stimulating environment where young learners can develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills.

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to promote learning through play, exploration, and hands-on activities. We emphasize language development, early math concepts, and social interaction.

Primary School

Overview

Our primary school curriculum is designed to build a strong foundation in core subjects like language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies.

Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that integrates academic learning with character development and critical thinking skills.

High School

Overview

Our high school program prepares students for college and beyond by offering rigorous academic courses and opportunities for personal growth.

Curriculum

Students in our high school follow a college-preparatory curriculum that includes Advanced Placement (AP) courses, honors classes, and opportunities for independent study.
